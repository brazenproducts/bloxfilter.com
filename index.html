FAQ

Common questions, answered cleanly.

How do I choose the right air filter? +

Start with exact size, then compare filtration level, airflow, and replacement cycle. The best pick is the one your system supports consistently.

Is a higher MERV rating always better? +

Not always. Higher filtration can help, but only if your HVAC system can handle it without restricting airflow too much.

Are bulk packs worth buying? +

Usually yes, if the fit and quality are consistent. They often provide the best long-term value for standard replacement schedules.
